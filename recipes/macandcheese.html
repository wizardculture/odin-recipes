Austin's Mac & Cheese!

About this recipe

The humble dish of macaroni and cheese is a wise choice if pursuing the finer things in life

Make this dish if you want to live luxuriously!

Ingredients

  • rigatoni
  • sharp cheddar
  • nutmeg
  • cayenne
  • milk
  • butter
  • flour
  • olive oil
  • love

Steps

  1. Create the rue with butter, olive oil and flour
  2. Add some milk and maybe a dash of nutmeg and cayenne
  3. After the milk has heated and started to cook, slowly add cheese while stirring
  4. You should've been boiling water and cooking the pasta by now! So do that if you haven't
  5. Combine pasta and cheese sauce. Let sit for a few minutes or bake it in the oven. Add spices as needed!
  6. Enjoy!
