Cayla's Cozy Potato Soup

About this recipe

Make this dish on a cozy winter evening as you ponder the meaning of it all!

Ingredients

  • PO TA TOS
  • Vegetables from the Shire
  • Milk from the cows roaming the fields near by

Steps

  1. Gather your crew of hobbits and go harvest potatos and vegetables
  2. Milk the local cows
  3. Get a large pot and combine
  4. Voila!
