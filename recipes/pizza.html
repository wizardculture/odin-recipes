Poppi's Woodland Pizza

About this recipe

Do you find yourself spending all day wandering the woodland realm, only to return home with cravings for delicious pizza? Me too! This recipe is for you.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato basil sauce
  • Artisanal cheese
  • Red onion
  • Pineapple!

Steps

  1. Wander the woodland realm until hungry
  2. Return home and knead some dough
  3. Add sauce
  4. Add cheese
  5. Add onion and pineapple
  6. Bake in your handmade pizza oven you built in order to make this recipe
  7. Enjoy!
